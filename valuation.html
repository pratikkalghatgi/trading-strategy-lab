Model Notes

The tool uses a classic free-cash-flow-to-firm DCF. It is educational and should be checked against filings.

FCFF = EBIT x (1 - tax rate) - reinvestment

Reinvestment = change in revenue / sales-to-capital

Terminal value = FCFF in year 11 / (WACC - terminal growth)

Banks, insurers, and REITs often work better with dividend or excess-return models than FCFF DCF.
