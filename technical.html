What this project is

Trading Strategy Lab is a browser-only educational tool. It combines strategy playbooks, indicator overlays, and a simple rule-based simulation engine.

How results are generated

Signals are produced by deterministic indicator rules in JavaScript. Results are approximate and designed for learning rather than production-grade execution modeling.

Educational only, not financial advice.
